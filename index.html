Ficly.org

Resources and Content from Ficly and ficlets

This site serves as a place to grab things related to Ficly and ficlets now that they're officially sunset (see the blog post in The Writer’s Room) and the static sites are live. If you'd like to do something with the stories created on either sites, there are files you can use in the two repos linked below.

Thank you to everyone who loved ficlets and Ficly and all the people who wrote stories, comments, and rated things. It was a joy to bring it to life.

Links

  • Ficly: It's now static.
    • Ficly Export: This is the Rails app used to generate the static version of Ficly. There is a SQL export of all the relevant tables in the db directory of this repo!
  • ficlets: The thing that came before Ficly. This is the static archive.
    • Ficlets Export: The Rails app used to generate the static version of ficlets.
